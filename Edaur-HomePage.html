Who are we ?

E-दौर, literally “The era of education” is Central India’s first student-based magazine startup that plans to educate and connect students from a city that is growing by leaps and bounds: Indore. As we contribute to the development of this educational hub, we are committed to providing the best for the leaders of tomorrow. Our magazine with meticulously prepared, engaging and precisely optimized content will be distributed freely to school and college-going students, quarterly. We also offer the advertiser and promoter an opportunity to highlight their services in front of the relevant audience.

About Us

E-Daur , is a magazine company in Indore

It believes in a meaningful entrepreneurial community, which can only thrive when the entire scope of the academic enterprise is located in close proximity, in order to foster the formal and informal inter communications that lead to productive collaboration.

VISION

We believe that every student has a tremendous potential to lead the nation that is only waiting to be unleashed. E-दौर is a one-stop resource that will contribute not only to academic studies but also to the overall personality enhancement of the individual. We are trying to ignite the spark in ambitious minds. We hope we can reach out to youngsters from every corner of the country soon!

MISSION

Hailing from a city that gravitates young talent from all over the country, we are a group of youngsters who have united with a mission to cater to every educational need of students, without gaining monetary benefit from them. We intend to support their effort to learn, discover, question and create through a huge plethora of knowledge that we devise carefully, every three months.
